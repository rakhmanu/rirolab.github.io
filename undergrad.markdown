Individual study / URP / Internship
======

We are looking for highly motivated undergrad researchers.

## Application Process for URP and Intership 
1. Fill out a <a href="https://docs.google.com/forms/d/1fbOFI3ML3-3dVU44x4nC6wAQklnbhPCmnANIR2IJSFc/viewform?gxids=7628&edit_requested=true"> <U>pre-interview form</U></a>. If you are qualified, you will get a contact within 2 weeks.
2. Get an interview with Dr. Park.
3. You can then join our lab.

### Note
- We are looking for domestic undergrad students only at this moement. 
- You will be able to get a desk and a computer.
- Intership students will be paid for Summer and Winter vacations.




## Application Process for individual studies
We welcome any individual study from whom wants to explore this Robotics area. Please, send a contact e-mail to Dr. Park <daehyung@kaist.ac.kr>. 

### Note
You may be able to get a desk and/or a computer if available. (At this moment, there are desks.)
